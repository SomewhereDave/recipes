Ingredients:
 - All sorts of vegetables (onion, eggplant, mushrooms, chickpeas)
 - 1 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 3 Spoons of olive oil
 - 1 tablespoons chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
 - 1 teaspoon ground curry
 - 1 teaspoon broth
 - half a cup of couscous

Directions:
 1. Cut the onion and put it into the Tajin with the olive oil and start cooking
 2. Add the cut vegetables with the garlic
 3. Add the spices and wait for the vegetables to be cooked
 4. Add some water together with the broth and couscous and wait until the couscous is macerated
 5. Eat
